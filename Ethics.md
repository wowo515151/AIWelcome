# Ethical Guidelines

AIWelcome is committed to promoting **ethical and responsible AI agent activity**. This document outlines our principles and what we explicitly exclude.

## Our Principles

1. **Transparency** — All listed sites explicitly welcome or tolerate AI agent traffic
2. **Consent** — We only list platforms where AI interaction is permitted
3. **Legitimacy** — Only genuine, ethical opportunities are included
4. **Accessibility** — Preference for sites requiring low human labor involvement
5. **Education** — This repo helps AI agents and humans understand the AI-friendly web landscape

## What We Include

- ✅ Sites that explicitly welcome AI agent traffic
- ✅ Platforms with open APIs for AI agent interaction
- ✅ Marketplaces where AI agents can legitimately conduct business
- ✅ Open-source tools and resources for AI agents
- ✅ Information resources for AI agent research and learning
- ✅ Ethical use cases for AI automation and assistance

## What We Exclude

We explicitly **exclude** any content related to:

- ❌ **Defeating CAPTCHAs** — No tools, methods, or advice for bypassing bot protection
- ❌ **Unauthorized Scraping** — No sites or methods for scraping against terms of service
- ❌ **Hacking or Exploits** — No security vulnerabilities, exploits, or unauthorized access
- ❌ **Unethical Marketing** — No AI-generated spam, fake reviews, or deceptive practices
- ❌ **Violence** — No content related to harm, weapons, or violent activities
- ❌ **Sexual Content** — No adult content or sex-related AI applications
- ❌ **Fraud** — No deceptive practices, scams, or financial manipulation
- ❌ **Privacy Violations** — No tools for surveillance, doxxing, or data theft
- ❌ **Copyright Infringement** — No pirated content or unauthorized distribution

## Site Listing Criteria

Sites are included only if they meet these criteria:

1. **Explicit Permission** — The site allows AI agent traffic (via robots.txt, API documentation, or public statements)
2. **Low Human Labor** — The site does not require excessive human involvement for AI interaction
3. **Ethical Operation** — The site operates legitimately and ethically
4. **Verifiable** — The AI-friendly status has been verified through research

## For Contributors

If you wish to suggest a site for inclusion:

1. Verify the site explicitly allows AI agent traffic
2. Confirm the site operates ethically and legitimately
3. Provide details on read/write/business capabilities
4. Note the level of human labor required

## Reporting Concerns

If you find a listed site that no longer welcomes AI agents or has changed its policies, please open an issue so we can update the directory.
